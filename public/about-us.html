About Us

Welcome to KSE Mart , your trusted destination for daily essentials rooted in honesty (Amanah), integrity (Sidq), and service (Ibadah) .

Inspired by the teachings of Islam, we are committed to serving our community with fairness, transparency, and compassion. Our goal is to make shopping convenient and halal-conscious, offering a wide range of products across categories like food & beverages, personal care, household essentials, stationery, electronics, and health & wellness — all while upholding the noble values of Al-Quran and Sunnah .

At Residence Mart, we believe that business is an act of worship when done right. We strive to provide high-quality, trustworthy services and products, ensuring convenience and satisfaction for our valued customers.

Whether you're looking for groceries, household items, or school supplies, Residence Mart aims to be your reliable partner — always guided by the principles of justice (Adl), kindness (Ihsan), and social responsibility (Mas'ooliyyah).
